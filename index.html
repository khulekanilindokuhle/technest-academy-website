Welcome to TechNest

TechNest is a coding and robotics class for kids, where creativity meets technology. We focus on hands-on learning, allowing children to build and program robots, preparing them for a future in STEM.

Our curriculum includes coding fundamentals, robotics engineering, computer literacy, and certifications from Cisco Network Academy.

We nurture problem-solving skills, logical thinking, and teamwork, making learning fun and engaging!

At TechNest, we foster creativity and critical thinking, helping kids become innovators in the tech world.

Our team is passionate about inspiring the next generation of tech leaders and preparing them for the challenges of tomorrow.

Coding Skills

Kids learn programming languages like Python, Scratch & C++, helping them develop essential coding skills for the future.

Robotics & AI

Building and programming real robots to solve challenges, and learning the basics of Artificial Intelligence (AI) for practical applications.

Future-Ready

Preparing young minds for careers in tech, innovation, and entrepreneurship with a strong foundation in cutting-edge technologies.

Teamwork & Collaboration

Our classes emphasize collaboration, where children work in teams to create projects, fostering teamwork and leadership skills.

Expert Mentors

Our instructors are experts in their fields, providing personalized guidance and mentorship to help kids succeed and reach their full potential.

Certifications & Achievements

Our students earn certificates from renowned organizations such as Cisco Network Academy, enhancing their resumes and future opportunities.

Scratch and Robotics

Unlock the potential of young minds by introducing them to programming with Scratch and robotics. In this class, students create engaging animations, games, and interactive stories using a visual programming language.   • Build and program robots
